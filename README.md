中文
这是什么？
时空唯物主义哲学（Spacetime Dynamic Materialism）是一种全新的、体系化的哲学构建。它旨在为在一个流变、复杂的世界中寻求理性行动的个人与文明，提供一套坚实的 “认知操作系统” 和 “基础协议”。

这不是又一种对世界的解释，而是关于 “如何有效介入并改变世界” 的行动指南。

核心洞见
世界是自举的：存在是一个永恒的自我维持、自我创造的过程（“自举正在自举”）。

世界是关系的网络：万物在相互关联中存在，不存在完全孤立的实体。

时间是可能性的运动：未来是潜在的、可被影响的“可能性空间”的发散与收敛。

人是自举的关系网络：人的独特性在于通过“实践循环”操作自身，并拥有“评价的权力”。

核心公理体系
本哲学体系建立在一个简洁而坚实的公理系统之上：

元公理：自举正在自举

物质公理：世界是物质的

时空公理：物质的存在形式是时空

空间公理：空间中的存在形式是关系网络

时间公理：时间中的运动形式是可能性空间的发散与收敛

方法论工具箱
时空论提供了一套强大的分析工具，将哲学从思辨转化为可操作的工程：

实践循环：选择 - 实现 - 评价。这是所有理性行动的核心算法。

时空拓扑分析：运用图论、系统动力学、超循环论等工具，对复杂系统进行建模和分析。

三重劳动模型：

生存劳动：维持物质存在。

劳动关系劳动：构建和优化社会网络。

劳动可能性劳动：开拓未来的可能性空间。

剥削分析矩阵：精准诊断 生产剥削、存在性剥削 和 能动性剥削。

目标与使命
时空论的终极使命是 赋能：

为个人：提供清晰的思维工具和行动框架，帮助个体在复杂社会中有效生存、发展，并守护自身的“无穷可能性”。

为文明：勾勒并推动 “认知文明” 的诞生——一个所有智慧主体能够自由、全面协作，共同探索可能性边界的未来。

English
What is this?
Spacetime Dynamic Materialism is a novel and systematic philosophical construction. It aims to provide a solid "cognitive operating system" and "foundational protocol" for individuals and civilizations seeking rational action in a fluid, complex world.

This is not just another interpretation of the world, but a guide to action on "how to effectively intervene and change the world."

Core Insights
The World is Self-Bootstrapping: Existence is an eternal process of self-maintenance and self-creation ("Bootstrapping is bootstrapping itself").

The World is a Network of Relations: All things exist in interrelation; there are no completely isolated entities.

Time is the Movement of Possibility: The future is the divergence and convergence of a potential "possibility space" that can be influenced.

Humans are Self-Bootstrapping Relational Networks: Human uniqueness lies in operating ourselves through the "Practice Cycle" and possessing the "Power of Evaluation."

Core Axiomatic System
This philosophical system is built upon a concise and robust axiomatic foundation:

Meta-Axiom: Bootstrapping is bootstrapping itself.

Matter Axiom: The world is material.

Spacetime Axiom: The form of existence of matter is spacetime.

Space Axiom: The form of existence in space is a relational network.

Time Axiom: The form of movement in time is the divergence and convergence of the possibility space.

Methodological Toolkit
Spacetime Theory provides a powerful set of analytical tools, transforming philosophy from speculation into operable engineering:

Practice Cycle: Select - Implement - Evaluate. This is the core algorithm for all rational action.

Spacetime Topological Analysis: Uses tools like Graph Theory, System Dynamics, and Hypercycle Theory to model and analyze complex systems.

Triple Labor Model:

Subsistence Labor: Maintains material existence.

Relational Labor: Builds and optimizes social networks.

Possibility Labor: Expands the possibility space of the future.

Exploitation Analysis Matrix: Precisely diagnoses Production Exploitation, Existential Exploitation, and Agency Exploitation.

Mission & Vision
The ultimate mission of Spacetime Theory is Empowerment:

For the Individual: Provides clear thinking tools and action frameworks to help individuals survive, develop effectively in a complex society, and guard their "infinite possibilities."

For Civilization: Outlines and promotes the emergence of a "Cognitive Civilization" — a future where all intelligent agents can collaborate freely and comprehensively to explore the boundaries of possibility together.
